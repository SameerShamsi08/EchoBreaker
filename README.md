
# EchoBreaker: The AI Advocate

An intelligent audio-processing agent designed to join meetings, listen to brainstorming sessions, and actively challenge your team's cognitive biases. EchoBreaker analyzes your strategies in real-time, searches the web for conflicting data or competitor failures, and presents a comprehensive "Devil's Advocate" report to ensure your ideas are bulletproof.

* **Audio Transcription:** Ingests live meeting audio or recorded transcripts to capture the core ideas being discussed.
* **Real-Time Fact-Checking:** Autonomously searches the web for data that contradicts the current consensus.
* **Competitor Analysis:** Pulls historical data on similar strategies that failed in your specific industry.
* **Bias Detection:** Identifies potential cognitive biases (e.g., survivorship bias, confirmation bias) in the team's logic.
* **Debrief Report:** Generates a structured, easy-to-read report outlining the strongest counter-arguments before you finalize a decision.
